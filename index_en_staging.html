　“Drone Impact Challenge ASIA CUP 2016”



    will be held in Main venue at Semboku, Akita, Japan.
    The race dates are July 29 to 31, 2016, with the Championships on Sunday, July 31 and qualifiers, fun racing, and elimination races all three days.

    The most of the activities will be in the Main Venue(Hotel Morinokaze Tazawako) area and Wing class championship racing will be held in the south side of the Dakigaeri valley for Oct 29 only.

    We will be held the Asian CUP for the first time in Japan.
    We will invite a pilot from Asian countries ( eight countries planned) . China , Korea , Singapore , Indonesia , etc.

    Date：July29(fri),30(Sat),31(Sun) 09:00～17:00　Rain or Shine
    Venue：Hotel Morinokaze Tazawako, Dakigaei Valley(Senboku-Shi, Akita, JAPAN)　www.morinokaze.com
    Contents：Keynote, Drone Race, Drone School, AND MORE.
